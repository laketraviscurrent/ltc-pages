How Advertising Works

We keep things intentionally simple.

Each issue features one primary sponsor, integrated naturally into the newsletter — not buried, not competing for attention.

Your placement feels more like a recommendation than an ad.

What You Can Expect

A monthly partnership, built for real visibility.

We work with partners on a monthly basis (four consecutive newsletters) to create consistency, familiarity, and real connection with our readers.

Each partnership includes:

  • 01

    "In Partnership With" logo placement

    Top of the newsletter — the first thing readers see when they open the issue.

  • 02

    Dedicated feature section

    Custom copy (≈100 words) paired with one image, written to fit the issue.

  • 03

    Business Spotlight

    Highlighting the owner, the story, or whatever makes your business distinct.

  • 04

    Website placement

    Homepage visibility plus a permanent spot on our partner page.

  • 05

    Editorial-style presentation

    Designed to feel native to the newsletter, not promotional.

We intentionally limit partnerships to maintain quality and avoid clutter. Each issue features a single primary partner.
